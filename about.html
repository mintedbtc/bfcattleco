Our Story

Building a legacy of excellence in cattle and Quarter Horses since 1985

Our Heritage

Located in the heart of Booneville, Arkansas, we've been dedicated to breeding superior cattle and Quarter Horses for over 40 years.

Our Mission

To produce the finest quality Angus cattle and Quarter Horses while maintaining the highest standards of animal care and breeding practices. We believe in preserving the rich heritage of ranching while embracing modern advancements in genetics and management.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, and dedication to our craft guide everything we do. We're committed to sustainable ranching practices and maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare.

Our Facility

Our state-of-the-art facility includes modern breeding facilities, training arenas, and extensive pastureland. We maintain the perfect environment for both cattle and horses to thrive.
