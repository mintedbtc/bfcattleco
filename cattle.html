Our Angus Program

We specialize in breeding high-quality Angus cattle with superior genetics for both commercial and registered operations.

Breeding Stock

Our breeding program focuses on producing cattle with excellent maternal traits, superior growth rates, and outstanding carcass quality. We carefully select our breeding stock based on EPDs, physical conformation, and performance records.

  • Superior maternal traits
  • Excellent growth rates
  • Outstanding carcass quality
  • Strong genetic selection

Genetics

Our herd represents some of the finest bloodlines in the Angus breed. We maintain detailed records of all breeding stock and their performance metrics to ensure continued improvement of our genetics.

  • Proven bloodlines
  • Performance-tested genetics
  • Comprehensive EPD data
  • Regular genetic evaluation

Management

We implement modern management practices to ensure the health and productivity of our herd. Our cattle receive regular veterinary care, nutritional monitoring, and performance tracking.

  • Regular health monitoring
  • Balanced nutrition program
  • Performance tracking
  • Quality assurance protocols
